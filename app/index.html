About Us

Daniel J. McElroy is a United States Air Force veteran, who flew C-141s in the Vietnam War.

He was a professional pilot for both Western Airlines and Delta Airlines before he retired in 2001. He currently has more than 20,000 hours in the air, and adds to it every day.

Dan is an ATP, CFI A&I Multi, and an A&P Mechanic. He's type Rated in B-727, B-777, MD-88, and Eclipse 500, and has flown over 67 types of airplanes.

He is well positioned to invent improvements to tow bars and other airplane support equipment, and patents these ideas for the use of airlines and other pilots.
